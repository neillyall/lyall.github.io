Almost-Periodicity of Convolutions

We are reading the following papers concerning the Croot-Sisask probabilistic approach for finding almost periodic convolutions (and applications):
  • A probabilistic technique for finding almost periodic convolutions, by Ernest Croot III and Olof Sisask
  • Arithmetic progressions in Sumsets and L^p-almost-periodicity, by Ernest Croot III, Izabella Laba and Olof Sisask
The following amazing papers utilize key ideas in the first paper above:
  • On Roth's theorem on progressions, by Tom Sanders
  • On the Bogolyubov-Ruzsa lemma, by Tom Sanders
